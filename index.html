Eco-revive:

A comprehensive waste management system that includes source segregation, collection, and disposal. The state promotes the decentralized processing of organic waste through biogas plants and composting units. Additionally, Kerala has established several material recovery facilities to manage recyclable waste and reduce landfill dependency, contributing to a more sustainable and environmentally friendly waste management approach.

ECO-REVIVE is classified into FOUR waste Management System

1.Biomedical waste

The biomedical waste management system in Kerala is governed by stringent regulations and guidelines to ensure safe and environmentally responsible handling of medical waste. It involves the segregation of biomedical waste at the source, proper packaging, and transportation to authorized treatment facilities.

2.E-Waste

The state promotes the responsible disposal and recycling of electronic products through designated collection centers and authorized recycling facilities. This system encourages the proper handling of e-waste, reducing environmental pollution and promoting resource recovery through the safe disposal and recycling of electronicÂ devices.

3.Construction Waste

Kerala has implemented a construction waste management system to handle the significant amount of waste generated in the construction and demolition industry.

4.Industrial waste

industrial waste management system to handle the waste generated by various industrial processes. The state enforces regulations to ensure industries properly manage and treat their waste. This includes the establishment of effluent treatment plants, solid waste disposal methods, and hazardous waste handling procedures.
